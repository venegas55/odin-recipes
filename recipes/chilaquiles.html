Hangover Killer Chilaquiles

Servings: 4

Prep Time: ~45m

Calories per serving: ~440

Ingredients

For the "salsa roja":

  • 3 guajillo chiles (seeded and deveined)
  • 3 ripe tomatoes (jitomates guaje)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ¼ white onion
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Optional: 1 sprig of epazote for extra flavor

For the Chilaquiles:

  • 10-12 corn tortillas (cut into triangles)
  • Oil for frying
  • 1 cup shredded cooked chicken (optional)
  • ½ cup Mexican crema
  • 1 cup queso fresco or Cotija, crumbled
  • ½ white onion, thinly sliced
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 2 fried eggs (optional but highly recommended)
  • Refried black beans (for serving)

Instructions

1.- Prepare the salsa roja:

Boil the tomatoes and guajillo chiles for 5-7 minutes until soft. Blend with garlic, onion, and a bit of the cooking water until smooth. Strain if desired. Sauté the salsa in a bit of oil, season with salt, and simmer for 5-10 minutes. Add epazote if using.

2.- Fry the tortillas:

Heat oil in a pan and fry tortilla triangles until golden and crispy. Drain on paper towels.

3.- Combine:

Add the fried tortillas to the simmering salsa and stir gently to coat. Cook for 2-3 minutes until slightly softened but still crisp at the edges.

4.- Serve:

Plate the chilaquiles and top with shredded chicken, crema, cheese, onion, cilantro, and a fried egg. Serve with refried beans on the side.
