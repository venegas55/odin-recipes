Traditional Italian Meat Lasagna

Servings: 12

Prep Time: ~1h 50m

Calories per serving: ~495

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 4 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 42 oz tomato purée
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 10 fresh basil leaves, torn

For the Béchamel Sauce:

  • 3 oz butter
  • 4 oz flour
  • 24 oz milk
  • ½ tsp salt
  • Dash of nutmeg
  • ¼ tsp white pepper

Others:

  • 1 lb lasagna noodles
  • 10 oz mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 tbsp grated Parmigiano Reggiano

Instructions

1.- Make the meat sauce:

Sauté garlic and parsley in olive oil. Add beef, season with salt, and cook until browned. Stir in tomato purée and simmer for 1 hour. Add basil at the end.

2.- Make the béchamel sauce:

Melt butter, stir in flour, and cook for 5 minutes. Gradually whisk in milk until smooth. Season with salt, nutmeg, and white pepper.

3.- Cook the noodles:

If using regular noodles, boil until just underdone. Rinse with cold water to stop cooking.

4.- Assemble the lasagna:

In a 9x13" pan, layer in this order: Thin layer of plain tomato sauce, lasagna noodles, béchamel sauce, meat sauce, mozzarella. Repeat for 4 layers, finishing with noodles, tomato sauce and parmesan.

5.- Bake:

Cover with foil (avoid touching the cheese and sauce) and bake at 400°F (200°C) for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until cheese is golden.

6.- Serve:

Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing. Serve with a side salad and garlic bread.
