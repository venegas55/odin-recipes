Tasty Valhalla Quinoa Recipe

This Quinoa concoction will cure all diseases and will make you feel like home

Freyja, the goddess loved this dish ❤️

Ingredients

  • Quinoa
  • Chicken broth
  • Asparagus
  • Cream cheese
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Boil water with a pinch of salt and let the quinoa boil for 15 minutes
  2. once it is soft, drain excess water and add the asparagus and the cream cheese
  3. Cook for 5 minutes or until all the cheese is mixed with all the other Ingredients
  4. On serve, add a little splash of olive oil to improve taste
  5. VOILA!, Tasty Valhalla Quinoa is ready!
