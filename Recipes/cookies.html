Almighty Cookies Recipe

Cookies for the younglings, exceptional taste and soft chocolate chips cookies

Nothing special yet they are the best

Ingredients

  • cookie dough
  • oven

Steps

  1. Roll the cookie dough in palm size balls
  2. Lay them on an oven flat pan with wax paper so they won't stick
  3. Cook in the oven for 25 minutes
  4. pour a glass of fresh cold milk and enjoy!
