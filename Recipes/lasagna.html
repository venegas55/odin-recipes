Almighty Lasagna Recipe

This tasty lasagna will bring back all the power and strength from the gods

Even though this is not a nordic recipe, i'm sure Odin would approve 👌

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Parsley leaves
  • Ground beef

Steps

  1. Boil water with a pinch of salt and let the lasagna paste on the water until it softens
  2. While it boils on a pan fry the ground beef until brown and add the tomato sauce
  3. Once the pasta and beef are ready, use an oven pan and lay a bed of pasta and cover it with the ground beef tomato concoction
  4. On the last layer of pasta use some parmesan cheese to grate on the oven and bake on a pre-heated oven for 45 minutes
  5. VOILA!, Almighty Lasagna is ready!
